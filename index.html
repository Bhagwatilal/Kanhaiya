Our Story

Every Dish Tells a Story

Since 2001, our fast food shop has been a local favorite, serving up authentic Indian street food with a passion for quality and flavor. From crispy Pani Puri to tangy Bhel Puri and creamy Dahi Puri, we've crafted each dish using fresh ingredients and time-honored recipes passed down through generations. For over two decades, we've been dedicated to delivering the same great taste and experience, making us a beloved destination for street food lovers in our town and beyond.
